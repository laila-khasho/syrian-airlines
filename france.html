Types of Visas to France

Tourist Visa

  • Valid Passport
  • Hotel Reservation
  • Travel Health Insurance
  • Bank Statement
  • Round-trip Flight Reservation

Student Visa

  • Acceptance from a French university or institute
  • Proof of financial ability
  • Health insurance
  • Personal photos
  • Copy of academic transcripts

Work Visa

  • Official job offer from a French company
  • Employment contract
  • Passport
  • Personal photos
  • CV

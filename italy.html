Types of Visas to Italy

Tourist Visa

  • Schengen Visa Application Form
  • Valid Passport
  • Two Passport Photos
  • Travel Health Insurance
  • Hotel and Flight Reservations
  • Proof of Financial Capability (Bank Statement)

Student Visa

  • Official acceptance from an Italian university or institute
  • Proof of financial ability or a scholarship
  • Health insurance
  • Proof of residence in Italy
  • Copies of academic certificates
  • Valid passport and photos

Work Visa

  • Official job offer from an Italian employer
  • Work permit issued by Italian authorities
  • Completed visa application
  • CV and experience certificates
  • Photos and a valid passport

About Us

Syrian Airlines was founded in 1946 and remains one of the first airlines in the region to offer distinguished services connecting East and West. Over the decades, the company has earned travelers' trust through its commitment to quality, safety, and passenger comfort. Many travelers have expressed their complete satisfaction with the company's services, praising the authentic Syrian hospitality, punctuality, and comfortable flight experience.

Types of visas to Switzerland

Tourist visa

  • Valid passport
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Hotel reservation
  • Travel health insurance
  • Round-trip flight reservation
  • Bank statement for the last 3 months

Study visa

  • Acceptance from an accredited university or institute in Switzerland
  • Proof of financial ability to cover costs
  • Comprehensive health insurance
  • Copies of academic transcripts
  • CV
  • Statement of purpose

Work Visa

  • Employment contract from a Swiss company
  • Updated CV
  • Experience or education certificates
  • Valid passport
  • Two personal photos

Types of Visas to the United States of America

Tourist Visa (B-2)

  • Valid Passport
  • Electronically completed DS-160 Form
  • Embassy-approved Digital Photograph
  • Visa Fee Payment
  • Schedule an Interview at the Embassy
  • Proof of Intent to Return (Family Ties, Employment, etc.)

Student Visa (F-1)

  • Form I-20 issued by a US educational institution
  • Proof of financial ability to cover tuition and living expenses
  • Pay the SEVIS fee
  • Complete Form DS-160
  • Schedule an appointment at the embassy and have an interview
  • Bring academic transcripts and resume

Work Visa (H-1B or other types)

  • Job Offer from a US Employer
  • Employment Petition Form Certified by the US Department of Labor
  • Valid Passport
  • Personal Photographs
  • Education and Experience Certificates
  • Complete Form DS-160

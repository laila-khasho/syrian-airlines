Types of Visas to China

Tourist Visa

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months
  • Two recent passport-size photographs with a white background
  • Completed and signed visa application form
  • Hotel reservation
  • Flight itinerary
  • Bank statement proving financial capacity

Student Visa

  • Acceptance Letter from a Chinese University or Institute
  • Student Visa Application Form (Form JW201 or JW202)
  • Passport and Personal Photos
  • Required Medical Examinations
  • Proof of Financial Capability or Scholarship

Work Visa

  • Official job offer from a company in China
  • Work permit issued by the Chinese government
  • Valid passport
  • Personal photos
  • Official medical examination
